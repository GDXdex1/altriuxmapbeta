# Agriculture Production System - Action Unit Requirements

## Time System

- **1 AU (Action Unit)** = 180 minutes blockchain time = 3 hours game time
- **1 Game Day** = 3 hours blockchain time (4x speed)
- **Worker Output** = 2 AU per game day = 8 AU per blockchain day

## Planting Requirements

### Land Parcels
- **Small Parcel** (1/4 xLand tile): **4 AU** = 12 hours blockchain = 2 game days
- **Farm Quarter** (building): **1 AU** = 3 hours blockchain = 0.5 game days

### Planting Process
1. Clear forest (if needed): 6 AU per parcel
2. Plow land: 2 AU per parcel
3. Plant seeds: 4 AU per parcel (small) or 1 AU (farm quarter)

## Crop Associations (Sequential Planting)

### Milpa System (Mesoamerican Three Sisters)
1. **Corn** (Maíz) - Plant first: 4 AU
2. **Beans** (Frijoles) - Plant 2 weeks later: 3 AU
3. **Squash** (Calabaza) - Plant 2 weeks after beans: 3 AU
- **Total**: 10 AU planting, 9 AU harvesting
- **Benefit**: +30% yield all crops, nitrogen fixation

### Mediterranean Rotation
1. **Wheat** (Trigo) - Plant first: 4 AU
2. **Peas** (Guisantes) - Intercrop: 2 AU
- **Total**: 6 AU planting, 5 AU harvesting
- **Benefit**: +20% wheat yield, soil nitrogen restoration

### Barley-Lentil Association
1. **Barley** (Cebada) - Plant first: 4 AU
2. **Lentils** (Lentejas) - Intercrop: 3 AU
- **Total**: 7 AU planting, 6 AU harvesting
- **Benefit**: +15% barley yield, weed suppression

### Rye-Vetch Cover Crop
1. **Rye** (Centeno) - Plant first: 4 AU
2. **Vetch** (Veza) - Intercrop: 2 AU
- **Total**: 6 AU planting, 5 AU harvesting
- **Benefit**: Erosion control, green manure

### Rice-Fish System (Asian)
1. **Rice** (Arroz) - Plant in paddies: 4 AU
2. **Fish** (Peces) - Stock 4 weeks later: 1 AU
- **Total**: 5 AU planting, 4 AU harvesting
- **Benefit**: +25% rice yield, fish protein, pest control

### Sugarcane-Bean Intercrop
1. **Sugarcane** (Caña) - Plant first: 5 AU
2. **Beans** (Frijoles) - Intercrop between rows: 3 AU
- **Total**: 8 AU planting, 8 AU harvesting
- **Benefit**: Maximize land use, nitrogen fixation

### Olive-Barley Agroforestry
1. **Olive Trees** (Olivos) - Perennial: 6 AU initial planting
2. **Barley** (Cebada) - Annual understory: 3 AU
- **Total**: 9 AU initial, 3 AU annual
- **Benefit**: Dual income, erosion control

### Banana-Cacao Shade System
1. **Banana** (Plátano) - Fast-growing shade: 3 AU
2. **Cacao** (Cacao) - Shade-loving crop: 4 AU
- **Total**: 7 AU planting, 6 AU harvesting
- **Benefit**: Optimal shade, dual tropical crops

### Clover-Cereal Rotation
1. **Clover** (Trébol) - Nitrogen fixer: 2 AU
2. **Wheat/Barley** - Following year: 4 AU
- **Total**: 6 AU over 2 years
- **Benefit**: Soil fertility restoration, livestock forage

## Seasonal Modifiers

### Spring (Primavera)
- Planting: -10% AU (faster soil work)
- Growth: Normal

### Summer (Verano)
- Planting: Normal
- Growth: +20% speed (more sunlight)
- Irrigation crops: -20% AU (easier water access)

### Autumn (Otoño)
- Harvesting: -10% AU (optimal conditions)
- Planting winter crops: Normal

### Winter (Invierno)
- Planting: +30% AU (frozen/hard soil)
- Growth: -50% speed (less sunlight)
- No planting in tundra/boreal

## Notes

- All AU requirements assume 1 worker
- Multiple workers can divide AU proportionally

- Association crops require compatible biomes
- Perennial crops have higher initial investment but long-term returns
